SANN

@

SANN (Sentiment Analysis with Neural Networks) is a simple tool that passes a Twitter user's most recent tweets through expertly developed models to determine if they have a "sentiment" about them:

  • Binary Sentiment: Positive or negative
  • Fine-Grained Sentiment: Very positive, slightly positive, neutral, slightly negative or very negative
  • Emotion Detection: Joy, love, surprise, fear or sadness
